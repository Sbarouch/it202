Rant by Sam Barouch on October 18, 2016.

Sleep is important

Tomorrow I get to take a nice long standardized test, but tonight is a different story. Tonight, I'm going to do three homework assignments, eat food and somehow find time to practive my violin.

Then, tomorrow I will have to finish up this project and do more homework assignments, then I'll finally be caught up in all my classes. Then I get to sleep.

Then, right after that there are midterms so I don't get to sleep anymore. Then I get to sleep after that.

People don't get enough sleep.
