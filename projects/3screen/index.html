Instructions


This app was designed so that someone with minimal technological experience and knowledge could utilize it. However, some explaining is still in order.
  • This application will sync itself with your device. If your phone or laptop has visited it before, you do NOT need an internet connection to access it later.
  • The to-do list is very self explanatory, however clicking an item will also strike it through, which may not be so apparent. Also, please note that the to-do list will not save what you write, so reloading the page or closing your browser will cause you to lose the list.
  • If you'd like me to change the daily reminders, just ask.
  • This website is also mobile-friendly. Should you browse on your phone, you will be able see the navigation menu on the left, rather than the top. Just click the button characterized by three lines.
